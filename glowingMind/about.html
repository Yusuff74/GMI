About us

Glowing Minds Initiatives For Human Development Is A Non For Profit Organization That Started In 2018 As An Organization To Address Youth Unemployment And Unemployability Through Entrepreneurship, Employability And Leadership Training, Climate Change Advocacy, Girl Child Education Advocacy, Civic Education And Advocacy All In A Bid To Attain Sustainable Development.

More About Us

Our activities seek to redress the issue of Unemployment in the state and the Nation at large. Burning issues like Lack of skills, enabling youth policies, infrastructure, and health issues and political which has continued to have devastating effects on community’s most especially young people, women, people with disabilities and children e.t.c.
