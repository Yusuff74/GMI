Sleta
Sleta

SLETA - The Students Leadership Entrepreneurship Technology and Agriculture Summit, started in 2018 at the Federal University of Technology, Minna is a weeklong capacity building workshop for undergraduates on campus Entrepreneurship. We have so far trained over 1000 beneficiaries across 10 Universities in 4 States in Nigeria.

project 600
project 600

This year, we are introducing Project 600, which is a project under our Students Leadership and Entrepreneurship Program, which aims providing 600 indigents undergraduates across six states of Nigeria access to entrepreneurship training and seed funding. This Project will help 600 indigents undergraduates stay in school, while helping to also helping to reduce the youth unemployment in Nigeria. We call on you to help us achieve this cause by partnering and sponsoring this project or better still you can donate to this cause.
